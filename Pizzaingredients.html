Ingredient glossary

Mozzarella
A mild cheese that melts smoothly and gives pizza its classic stretch.
Pepperoni
A spicy, cured meat that crisps at the edges when baked.
Pesto
A sauce made with herbs (often basil), garlic, oil, and cheese—bright and fragrant.
Mushrooms
Earthy flavor; slicing thin helps them cook evenly and avoid excess moisture.
